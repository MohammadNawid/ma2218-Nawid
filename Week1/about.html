About Us - Earthrunners

Earthrunners was brought to life by two enthusiastic world travelers, Emma Johanson and Jake Roberts, who share a deep belief in the life-changing potential of travel. Our core purpose is to deliver extraordinary and adventurous travel experiences that transcend the ordinary.

Our Story

In the bustling city of Chicago, two strangers, Emma and Jake, serendipitously met and bonded over their shared passion for travelling. Inspired by their adventures, they founded Earthrunners, a travel agency with a mission to curate transformative experiences. Their journey took them to stunning destinations worldwide. With Emma's photography skills and Jake's technical expertise, Earthrunners was born.
